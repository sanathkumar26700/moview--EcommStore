
# MOVIEW
### E-commerce store for Compact disks 

The feeling of fulfilment and pride of holding the original DVD case of your favourite movie and placing it in your showcase as a piece of artifact is immeasureable, with the rise of OTT nowadays we have forgotten this feeling â˜¹

So to bring back the lost glory of CDs, MOVIEW - an E-commerce store where you can find Blu rays, DVDs & VCDs of your favourite movies, series and movie collections !!


## TECH-STACK

 [View.I](https://viewi.netlify.app/) - component library.
 
 ReactJS
 
 React Router
 
 [mockbee](https://mockbee.netlify.app/) for backend
